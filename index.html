## Project Summary - Optimizing Public Transportation for Sustainability

We analyzed a public transport bus dataset with over 1 million records. Our objective was to use AI to suggest optimizations for reducing fuel usage and carbon footprint.

### Key Findings:
- Cluster 0 contains high-demand stops. These areas need increased bus frequency.
- Cluster 4 has low average boardings. Routes through these stops can be reduced or merged.
- Top 10 busiest routes contribute to majority of boardings — optimizing their scheduling will enhance fuel efficiency.

### Suggestions:
- Increase buses for high-boarding clusters during peak times.
- Reduce/merge low-demand routes to save fuel.
- Use AI clustering regularly to update and optimize routes based on current demand.
